Hello! My name is

Zachary Jordan

I write beautiful and functional apps and websites in order to make people's lives more enjoyable and simple. I follow an organized process when I create:
Develop
I approach each project as if design and programming are one and the same. This enables my projects to be as visually appealing and easy to use as they are functional.
Research
I do thorough research into what each project requires before I even begin programming. This allows me to create programs that are adaptive to the needs of each individual effort.
Deploy
I have been involved with deployment on a variety of platforms to ensure that I'm familiar with it all. Wherever my project can best reach people is where that project ends up.

Don't Touch Anything
Don't Touch Anything is a game about dodging obstacles and collecting coins. It's simple, fast, and fun. This was my first iPhone app, and the first game that I actually decided to make public.

Student Companion
Student Companion is an app that grants students easy access to a wide variety of tools to make their educational experience more streamlined and fun. It provides everything from test and grade calculation tools to tips that help students succeed on the ACT.

Lunarcy - A Moon Exploration
Lunarcy is an interactive educational experience that walks curious users through a guided tour of our closest natural satellite. It allowed me to practice my animation and design skills and challenged me to keep the content at an easily digestable level.
This app is currently offline.
Easy Sleep
Easy Sleep is an app that seeks to make groggy mornings a thing of the past. It uses precise algorithms to estimate the duration of an individuals REM sleep to pinpoint the optimal time for said individual to wake up.
This app is coming soon.
The Cure It Project
The product of over a year of development, The Cure It Project mobile app seeks to reinvent public access to information regarding cancer by making it simple to understand and enjoyable to learn about.
This app is coming soon.
The Sea
The Sea is an interactive exploration of the Mariana Trench, the deepest currently known point in the sea. The entire trench is represented to scale, meaning that the markers that denote the current depth are true to how deep the trench actually is in real life.

The Cure It Project
The Cure It Project desktop website is a collection and simplification of the most powerful resources currently avaliable to learn about and combat the threat of cancer. It serves to instruct and inform users through a single medium, and was my first experience with a long term programming project.
